Privacy Policy for Grammar Corrector

Effective Date: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Grammar Corrector ("the Extension") is designed with your privacy as the highest priority. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Extension.

2. Zero Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or the text you highlight. All operations are performed locally on your machine.

3. Local AI Processing

The Extension uses Google Chrome's built-in AI (Gemini Nano) to analyze and correct your text. Because this AI is built directly into your browser, all text processing happens entirely on your local device. Your highlighted text is never sent to our servers or any external third-party cloud servers.

4. Required Permissions

To function properly, the Extension requires the following standard browser permissions, which are used strictly for local functionality:

  • Context Menus: Allows the Extension to add a "Correct Grammar" option when you right-click on highlighted text.
  • Active Tab & Scripting: Allows the Extension to read the specific text you highlighted and replace it with the AI-corrected version.
  • Storage: Allows the Extension to save your chosen settings (like your preferred English dialect and editing mode) locally on your browser.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Chrome: Because the Extension relies on Chrome's built-in AI capabilities, usage of the AI is subject to Google's standard privacy policies.
  • Donations: The Extension contains a link to PayPal for optional donations. The Extension does not process or have access to any of your financial or payment information.

6. Changes to this Policy

If we make any significant changes to how we handle your data, we will update this Privacy Policy and modify the "Effective Date" at the top.
